About Me

Joel Mwangala is an IT Specialist with over four years of experience, blending technical expertise with strategic thinking. With a background in Environmental Planning and an ongoing Master’s in Strategic Management, he approaches problem-solving from both a practical and visionary lens.

In his current role at a Christian NGO, Joel developed a static website using AWS, HTML, and JavaScript to streamline access to training resources for field teams—demonstrating his initiative and commitment to user-centered solutions.

He is certified in IT Support and is currently expanding his cloud expertise through the ALX Cloud Computing program. His core strengths include troubleshooting, cloud infrastructure, and crafting solutions that are both scalable and impactful.

Joel is also the founder of FaithStack.Dev, a movement rooted in the belief that technology can be a powerful force for good. FaithStack.Dev exists to inspire and equip people to solve problems boldly by taking the next step forward in faith.

He is currently seeking opportunities to contribute to mission-driven organizations where he can leverage technology to create lasting, strategic impact.
